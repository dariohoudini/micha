## 2. Data we collect

### Account data
- Email address, phone number, username

- Profile photo, city, date of birth (optional)

### Seller data
- Angolan national ID (BI number) — encrypted at rest, used for identity verification only
- Bank account details — encrypted at rest, used for payments only
- Business information

### Transaction data
- Orders, payments, shipping addresses
- Chat messages with other users

### Usage data
- Products viewed, searched, added to cart
- Device type, browser, approximate location

### Technical data
- IP address, session identifiers
- Error logs (anonymised after 90 days)

## 4. Your rights under Lei 22/11

You have the right to:
- **Access** — request a copy of all your personal data
- **Correction** — fix inaccurate data
- **Erasure** — delete your account and associated data
- **Portability** — receive your data in a machine-readable format
- **Objection** — object to processing based on legitimate interest
- **Withdraw consent** — at any time, for consent-based processing

To exercise your rights: privacy@micha.app or Settings → Data & Privacy in the app.
We respond within 30 days.
